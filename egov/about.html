Welcome to the Department of Passports

The Department of Passports (DoP) is the official government agency responsible for issuing and managing passports for Nepali citizens. We are committed to ensuring the smooth, secure, and efficient processing of passport applications and facilitating the travel of Nepali citizens across the globe.

Our Mision

Our mission is to provide reliable and secure passport services to Nepali citizens, ensuring that every individual can travel internationally with confidence and ease. We aim to enhance the efficiency of our processes while upholding the highest standards of security.

Our Vision

To be a leading government agency that ensures seamless, efficient, and secure travel for Nepali citizens by providing modern passport services in a transparent, accessible, and timely manner.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We operate with transparency, honesty, and accountability, maintaining the trust of the citizens we serve.
  • Security: We ensure that all passports are issued with the highest level of security and fraud prevention measures.
  • Efficiency: We continuously improve our services to provide faster processing times and better service for our citizens.
  • Accessibility: We are committed to making passport services accessible to all Nepali citizens, regardless of location or background.

What We Do

The Department of Passports provides a range of services to Nepali citizens, including:

  • Issuance of Passports: We issue standard and diplomatic passports to Nepali citizens.
  • Passport Renewal: We assist citizens in renewing expired or nearing-expiry passports.
  • Emergency Travel Documents: We offer emergency travel documents to citizens who are abroad and require immediate travel solutions.
  • Lost or Stolen Passports: We assist citizens with reporting lost or stolen passports and provide guidance on obtaining replacements.
  • Visa & Immigration Assistance: We provide information on travel-related documents for international travel.
